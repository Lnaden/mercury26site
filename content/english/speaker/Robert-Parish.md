As a numerical scientist, all that matters is to see your codes used to tackle real-world problems.

Rob Parrish's career is built around the idea that solving the quantum mechanical Schrödinger equation faster can help to computationally design better molecules and materials. In pursuing that, He's worked with really talented groups using CPUs, GPUs, and even QPUs to bring the codes to the next level.
